Competitive Gameplay (Landscape)

Key Elements

  • Dual HUD: P2 score (blue, left) — Timer (center) — P1 score (orange, right)
  • Dashed divider line splits the garden
  • Each player has own pots, tools, seeds in their zone
  • P1 glove = orange glow, P2 glove = blue glow
  • Cross-zone harvest goes to zone owner
  • Golden Watering Can appears when score gap > 20%

Round End (1P / Co-op)

Key Elements

  • Always positive title ("Beautiful Garden!")
  • Score + Plants Grown boxes
  • Random encouraging message
  • Chapter Complete banner (when applicable)
  • "Keep Growing" (next level) or "Start Over"
  • No failure language ever
