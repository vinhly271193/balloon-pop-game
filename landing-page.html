Traditional therapy can feel repetitive and clinical. Patients disengage. Garden Grow transforms physical and cognitive exercises into a joyful, nature-themed experience that patients look forward to.

Therapeutic Design

Built on Four Pillars of Therapeutic Care

Every element of Garden Grow is designed around evidence-based therapeutic principles.

🤲

Physical Activity

Hand-tracking transforms reaching, grasping, and sweeping motions into natural gardening movements. Supports seated and standing play with no controllers required.

🧠

Cognitive Stimulation

Progressive challenges engage attention, processing speed, and task completion without relying on memory or complex rules. Every task is clearly displayed on screen.

👥

Social Engagement

Two-player modes designed for intergenerational pairs. Grandmother with grandson, patient with therapist. Connection over correction.

🌿

Wellbeing Support

No failure states. Every interaction is rewarded. Gentle pacing, positive feedback, and the calming theme of a growing garden promote relaxation and joy.

Capabilities

Designed for Therapeutic Settings

Every feature is built with care professionals and their patients in mind.

🎯

Dynamic Difficulty

The game monitors each player and adjusts challenge level every 5 seconds. No manual settings needed. Each player gets their own difficulty curve.

🤝

1 or 2 Players

Solo play, cooperative (Community Garden), or friendly competition (Village Fair). All modes celebrate success. No one loses.

✨

No Failure States

Every round ends in celebration. Help appears naturally when needed. No "game over" screens. No pressure. No penalty for wrong touches.

♿

Fully Accessible

WCAG AA compliant. High contrast modes. Large targets (70px+). 3-second dwell activation. No fine motor skills required.

🎵

Synthesized Audio

Gentle, non-startling sounds generated in real-time with the Web Audio API. Adjustable volume. Can be fully muted for quiet environments.

⚡

Zero Setup Time

Staff setup in under 30 seconds. No accounts, no logins, no configuration, no downloads. Just open the browser and play.

Research

Evidence-Based Design

Garden Grow's therapeutic framework is grounded in published dementia research and clinical best practices.

Non-pharmacological interventions including physical activity, cognitive stimulation, and social engagement are key modifiable risk factors for dementia prevention and management.

Lancet Commission on Dementia Prevention, Intervention, and Care, 2024
🛡️

Errorless Learning

Based on research showing dementia patients benefit from task designs that prevent errors rather than correct them. No wrong answers, no failure states.

🧩

Multi-Domain Stimulation

Combines motor, cognitive, and social engagement in a single activity — the approach with the strongest evidence base for therapeutic outcomes.

🌟

Self-Efficacy Through Success

Positive feedback loops build confidence and motivation, increasing engagement and session duration over time. Every round ends in celebration.
