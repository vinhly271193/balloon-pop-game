Garden Grow — Accessibility Preview

re:Action Health Technologies
Demonstrating WCAG-compliant contrast modes across all game screens
Normal

Default Mode

Optimized for comfort and visual warmth. Garden-themed color palette with soft gradients. Meets WCAG AA standards with a minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio on all text.

High Contrast

Enhanced Visibility

All text becomes bolder. Backgrounds darken for stronger separation. Borders thicken on interactive elements. Colors intensify. Designed for moderate visual impairment.

Maximum Contrast

Near Black & White

Pure white text on pure black backgrounds. No gradients, no transparency. Primary actions use #00FF00 green on black. Borders at maximum visibility. For severe visual impairment.

Designed for dementia patients in therapeutic settings. No fine motor requirements. All interactions work with gross hand movements via webcam. Button activation uses a 3-second dwell hover system with visual progress ring, eliminating the need for clicking or tapping. Touch targets exceed WCAG minimum of 44px at 70px+. No failure states -- every round ends with positive reinforcement.
